About ForgivEase

ForgivEase is a wellness and spiritual self-help app for structured forgiveness practice. It offers reflection prompts, emotion ratings, local notes, optional voice notes, reminders, quotes, meditation practices, safety guidance, and local preview circle timers.

Wellness Scope

ForgivEase is a private reflection tool. Professional care, emergency services, legal advice, medical care, therapy, and crisis support are separate resources.

Use emergency support or a trusted professional when there is immediate danger, violence, abuse, self-harm risk, severe distress, medical need, legal risk, or coercion.

User Responsibility

Users choose how to use each practice. ForgivEase never requires reconciliation, contact with another person, disclosure of private stories, spiritual identification, or release of practical boundaries.

Users remain responsible for external decisions, safety planning, communication, legal decisions, medical decisions, and professional support.

Local Data

The current MVP stores practice history on the user's device. Users can remove a saved process, remove a voice note, clear a draft, and delete all local data from Settings.

Users should keep their device secure. Mobile devices can be lost, shared, unlocked, backed up, or inspected by someone with access to the device.

Voice Notes And Reflections

Voice notes and written reflections may contain personal content. Users should record and write only what feels appropriate for a private mobile device.

ForgivEase local exports are user-controlled. When users share exported text through the system share sheet, the chosen destination controls that copy.

Community Preview Scope

The current Circle area is a local MVP preview. Silent Forgiveness Circles and Global Peace Minute currently provide selection, intention text, and timer behavior inside the app.

Future group rooms, moderation, reporting, blocking, aggregate counters, and shared participation features will require additional terms and safety controls before public launch.

Future Paid And AI Features

The current MVP has no paid content and no live AI processing. Future AI, image, payment, supporter, free-access, or facilitator features will require updated terms, privacy language, store review notes, and user consent flows.

Intellectual Property

ForgivEase app design, text, structure, prompts, and original content belong to the developer or licensed contributors. Users keep responsibility for their own reflections, notes, and voice recordings.

Availability

ForgivEase may change, pause, or remove development features during MVP testing. Internal builds may expire, require a development server, or stop working when the build profile changes.
