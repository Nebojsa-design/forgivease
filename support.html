Urgent Help

ForgivEase support cannot provide emergency help, crisis response, medical care, legal advice, or protection services. In urgent danger, self-harm risk, violence, abuse, coercion, or severe distress, contact local emergency services, a crisis line, a trusted person, or a qualified professional now.

What Support Can Help With

  • Questions about app navigation and practice flows.
  • Issues with reminders, notification permission, or local settings.
  • Issues with optional voice notes, microphone permission, or playback.
  • Questions about local data deletion and local export behavior.
  • Bug reports about crashes, frozen screens, or unclear app behavior.
  • Questions about privacy, Terms of Use, or current MVP scope.
